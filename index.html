Zoids: Chaotic Century

Zoids: Chaotic Century is a Japanese anime series that is part of the Zoids franchise. It is set in a world where mechanical creatures called Zoids battle for supremacy. The series follows the journey of Van Flyheight, a young pilot who seeks to uncover the mysteries of the Zoid Eve and protect his friends from the dangers that lurk in their world.
